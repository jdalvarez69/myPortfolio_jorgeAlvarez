AI-Assisted Workflow

I use AI tools like ChatGPT as collaborative partners in my workflow, not as replacements for thinking. Here's how:

1

Plan & Define

I start with clear requirements, questions, and goals. I write detailed prompts that reflect actual thinking, not vague requests.

2

Collaborate

I treat AI as a thinking partner. I review outputs critically, ask follow-up questions, challenge assumptions, and iterate.

3

Adapt & Improve

I take AI suggestions and make them my own. I modify, refine, and personalize the work to match my vision and voice.

4

Verify & Own

I take full responsibility for the final output. I check accuracy, test functionality, and ensure quality before shipping.

Why this matters: Using AI well is a professional skill. It's about combining human judgment, critical thinking, and AI capabilities to work better. I'm not hiding AI use—I'm showing that I understand how to use it responsibly as part of serious work.
